Linked exercises

A new Linked exercises section on the piece detail, the multi-select picker that fills it, and the genuinely new piece — the 0–10 score ring (#1009) that retires the 5-step bars everywhere a mastery signal appears.

Decided with you
  • Ring: monochrome arc gauge, numeral centred — 4 treatments below to confirm.
  • Row density: title + key/tempo + ring only. No last-practised / best-tempo clutter.
  • Score 0: empty ring, faint en-dash, never a numeral.
  • Picker: both multi-select patterns drawn (A / B).
Reused, not reinvented

ScreenScaffold, .cardSurface(), HairlineDivider, the type-coded bar (gold = exercise), TypeBadge and the TagFilterSheet chrome all carry over unchanged. The ring reuses the existing score-indigo — no new tokens. The MasteryMeter bars are replaced by the ring on library rows and on the exercise detail.

The one new component · #1009

Score ring · 0–10

Monochrome on cream, numeral centred. Value is encoded twice — by the fill and by the numeral — so it reads with no colour at all. Four treatments at the same score (7) for a fair look; pick one and I'll standardise it.

My pick · A
Arc gauge
Proportional sweep, round cap. Calm, glanceable, scales tiny.
Segmented
Ten ticks = literal 0–10. Countable, very accessible.
Wedge
Pie fill, boldest read. Heavier; less calm at small sizes.
Dial marker
Static ring, serif numeral leads. Calmest, most typographic.
