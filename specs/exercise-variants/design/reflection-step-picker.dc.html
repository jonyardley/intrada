Step picker on the hand-off sheet

A row of tap-to-select chips — an input, so SegmentedPills apply here (unlike the display-only ladder). Defaults to the current step, already selected. A user who just taps Save & continue never touches it — zero added interaction. One tap swaps the selection to any other step; nothing else on the sheet moves. Placed between Score and Tempo, since the target tempo the stepper prefills depends on which step was practised. An exercise with no ladder shows nothing new — see the shipped sheet in Reflection Narrative · Surface 2, unchanged.
